Avionics Maintenance Technician
BAE Systems 12·2013 - Present

In my current role I carry out routine servicing, fault investigation, defect rectification and modification embodiment in accordance with EASA Mil Part 45 regulations. These tasks encompass strip and de-looming of avionic bays, wire deletes, installation and re-looming of new fibre optic, coaxial and electrical cables, rebuild, and functional testing of avionic and electrical systems including: Navigation, Flight Control, Communications, Radar, Electrical Power and Armament Systems.
